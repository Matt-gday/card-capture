Rules

Objective

An evil AI is transferring robots from a factory. Capture all face cards (robots) before the AI does. If even one face card enters the AI's capture pile, you lose.

Setup

Your deck: All 2s, 3s, 4s, and both Jokers (14 cards).
AI deck: Everything else (40 cards). Four cards dealt face-up. Any face cards dealt during setup go to the bottom.

Card Values

2–10Face valueJack11Queen12King13Ace14JokerCopies any card in hand

Round Phases

1. AI Phase: Cards slide right. Empty slots refill from AI deck.

2. Discard: Discard any cards from your hand.

3. Draw: Draw up to 4 cards. Empty deck → shuffle discards.

4. Capture: Do one action:

Capture: Same-suit hand cards totalling ≥ enemy card value.

Offer: Give one hand card to position 1 (rightmost). Both go to AI pile. Can't if position 1 is a face card.

Sacrifice: Give AI two hand cards. Send one enemy row card to bottom of AI deck.

Jokers

Copy the suit and value of any other card in your hand.

Win / Lose

Win: Clear AI deck and row with no face cards captured by AI.

Lose: A face card enters the AI capture pile.
